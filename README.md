# Digital-Literacy Project

This repository contains my Digital Literacy project work completed as part of my coursework. It includes five tasks that focus on understanding digital tools, online safety, communication, and professional presence.

## Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
In this task, I created a one-page infographic to explain what digital literacy means and why it is important for students. I used Canva to design it and included topics like useful tools, safe internet practices, and email etiquette.

## Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
For this task, I created and updated my profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. This helped me understand how to build a professional online presence and how these platforms are useful for future opportunities.

## Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
In this task, I practiced coding on HackerRank by solving a beginner-level problem. I also created a Google Form quiz to understand how collaboration tools can be used for collecting and analyzing responses. Here is the link to my google forms quiz-  https://forms.gle/9XYPnQLrb8BuiucdA

## Task 4 – Professional Email & Etiquette
This task involved writing formal emails and learning proper email etiquette. I also created a checklist of social media do’s and don’ts to promote responsible online behavior among students.

## Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention
In this task, I explored a case study on cybercrime and understood how such incidents happen. I also created a prevention checklist to spread awareness about staying safe online and avoiding common scams.

## Conclusion
Overall, this project helped me understand the importance of digital literacy in both academic and real-life situations. It improved my awareness of online safety, communication, and professional skills.
